Simple bulk product stock updater plugin for Woocommerce

== Description ==
Bulk Product Stock Manager for Woocommerce is a simple stock updater, just select the categories and put quantity it will update products in instant.

The plugin keeps the history of every product stock whenever you update the quanity, its easy to restore your old stock incase you go wrong :)
